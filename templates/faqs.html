Daily Drop FAQs

How do I place an order?

+

To place an order, simply browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your delivery address, and confirm payment.

What are the delivery charges?

+

Delivery is free for all orders.

How long will my order take?

+

Orders are typically delivered within 10-15 minutes during operating hours. Peak times may affect delivery times slightly.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We currently accept only cash on delivery.

What is your return/refund policy?

+

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 2 hours of delivery to receive a refund or replacement.

How can I contact customer support?

+

You can reach us via in-app chat, email us at support@dailydrop.com, or call +91-12345-67890.
