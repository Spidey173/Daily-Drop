Our Story

From a Simple Idea to Revolutionizing Daily Shopping

DailyDrop was founded by four friends who were frustrated with the time-consuming process of grocery shopping. We asked ourselves: what if you could get your daily essentials faster than it takes to brew a cup of coffee?

Our mission was clear - to create a service that delivers not just products, but time back to our customers. We built an innovative logistics platform, partnered with local suppliers, and assembled a dedicated team to make the impossible possible.

Today, DailyDrop delivers thousands of products to happy customers across the city in just 10-15 minutes. We're more than a delivery service - we're your reliable partner for everyday convenience.
